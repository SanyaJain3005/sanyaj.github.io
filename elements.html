Evercore Partners

Investment Banking summer associate

As an Investment Banking Summer Associate, I contributed to six high-impact transactions, including a ~$3B buy-side logistics deal and a ~$200M sell-side process for a sports graphics and tech provider. I developed revenue projections, expense analyses, and financial models, driving decision-making and facilitating successful deals. My responsibilities included conducting detailed operational data analysis to identify strategic opportunities, performing valuation methodologies like DCF and Comparable Companies, and preparing comprehensive pitch presentations for a $300B+ client. Additionally, I collaborated on crafting buyer profiles, CIMs, and management presentations to support the sell-side process.

ATG Samata

VC Internship - Climate Space

At ATG Samata, I formulated an investment thesis centered on Climate Adaptation and Mitigation strategies across the African continent, with a primary focus on Kenya and Nigeria. Leveraging in-depth research and analysis, I also developed a country-specific investment thesis for Mozambique, identifying it as a high-potential investment destination. By evaluating critical factors such as political stability, economic trends, inflation, currency performance, and the broader business outlook, I provided actionable insights that highlighted Mozambique's untapped opportunities. These recommendations influenced the firm's strategic direction, enabling more informed and impactful investment decisions in sustainable initiatives across the region.

Prath Ventures

VC Internship - Consumer Space

At Prath Ventures, as part of the deal team, I actively sourced startups and conducted due diligence on startups, delivering detailed investment recommendations to partners. I also developed an investment thesis for the Indian Gaming industry, providing a comprehensive analysis of the industry outlook, key trends, and growth potential. This included identifying investment opportunities in three seed-stage gaming startups and assessing associated risks. My contributions played a critical role in targeting high-growth sectors and aligning the firm's investments with strategic market opportunities.

Procter & Gamble

I worked with with products at different life stages - I lauched products from 0 to 1, I scaled them from 1 to 10 and I also worked with products that were there in the market for decade.
